Variable: Definition
Number of step: represents a unit of time where 1 step equals 1 hour
Type of Transaction: type of online transaction
Amount: the amount of the transaction
Customer Name: customer starting the transaction
Balance Before Transaction':customer's balance  before the transaction
Balance After Transaction': customer's balance after the transaction
Recipient Name: recipient of the transaction
Recipient Old Balance: initial balance of recipient before the transaction
Recipient New Balance: the new balance of recipient after the transaction
